Privacy Policy for Quel

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Introduction

Quel (“the App”) is an engineering toolkit designed to assist users with valve sizing, relief valve sizing, orifice calculations, and unit conversions. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

We respect your privacy. Our goal is to be transparent about what data we do and do not collect.

Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data: The App does not collect, store, or share personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account details.
  • No Tracking: The App does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or analytics tools.
  • Local Calculations Only: All calculations are performed locally on your device. No input values or results are transmitted to servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party services that collect or process user data.

Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit user data, there are no associated data storage or transfer risks. All activity remains on your device.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for professional and educational use. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
